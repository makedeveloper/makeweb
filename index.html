Introduction

MakeDeveloper is a platform for the students interested in Software Development with a purpose to build a better developer culture, building some interesting projects, which will be useful for society, organisations and to improve participants' knowledge and prepare them to enter the Open Source community We are working on several projects covering important fields of computer science like Machine Learning , Android app development , Web development , Blockchain , Cyber security etc. , where our mentors are guiding students in making their projects.
